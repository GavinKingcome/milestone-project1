In the heart of King's Cross lies a small oasis

Camley Street Natural Park is a hidden gem in the heart of London. Running beside the Regent's canal it offers a unique blend of urban wildlife and nature, making it a perfect escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.

A variety of habitats, including ponds, meadows, and woodlands, provide a home for diverse wildlife. Whether you're looking to relax, explore, or learn about nature, Camley Street has something to offer.

Join us for events, workshops, and guided walks to discover the beauty of nature right in the city. Our friendly staff are here to help you reconnect with the natural world.
